# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct.

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy
and kindness, being respectful of differing opinions, giving and gracefully accepting
constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, publishing
others' private information without permission, and other conduct that could reasonably
be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported using the email: [code_of_conduct@mcpdesc.org](mailto:code_of_conduct@mcpdesc.org) 
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 is available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
